Frequently Asked Questions


Here are the frequently asked questions that people enquired online before choosing a virtual office. We have gathered the most crucial ones and answer here to help you in making the right decision.


What is a coworking space?
A coworking space is a shared workspace in which people from different professional backgrounds work together. It gives you an amazing work environment and access to numerous state of the art amenities resulting in better communication, productivity, creativity and innovation.
Who uses coworking spaces? <
From small startups to SMEs and large corporations, every type of organization can utilize a coworking space. In fact, individuals like business travelers, freelancers, remote workers, solo entrepreneurs and content creators etc. are also utilizing modern coworking spaces for their business needs.
How much does a coworking space cost?
The overall cost of a coworking space depends on the type of seats you choose- Hot Desks, Dedicated Desks, Private Cabins and the location where it is located.
